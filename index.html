Indian Architecture & History

Where time speaks through stones and carvings.

Ancient Temples of India

Architectural Marvels Carved in Stone

Explore Sacred Architecture

Journey through centuries of devotion and craftsmanship, where each temple tells a story of faith, art, and engineering excellence.

Kailasa Temple

8th century monolithic wonder carved from a single rock, dedicated to Lord Shiva at Ellora Caves.

Konark Sun Temple

13th century architectural masterpiece designed as a colossal chariot with intricate stone carvings.

Shore Temple

8th century granite masterpiece overlooking the Bay of Bengal at Mahabalipuram.

Brihadeeswarar Temple

11th century Chola dynasty marvel in Thanjavur, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Cultural Heritage of India

A tapestry woven through millennia, blending art, architecture, philosophy, and traditions that continue to inspire the world.

UNESCO World Heritage Sites

44 sites including Taj Mahal, Ajanta Caves, Hampi monuments, and many more architectural marvels.

Classical Arts

Eight classical dance forms, including Bharatanatyam and Kathak, preserving ancient storytelling traditions.

Ancient Texts

Vedas, Upanishads, and epics like Mahabharata and Ramayana guiding philosophy and ethics.

Architectural Legacy

From stepwells to forts, temples to palaces, showcasing diverse regional styles and innovations.

Living Traditions

Yoga, Ayurveda, and meditation practices that have gained global recognition and practice.
